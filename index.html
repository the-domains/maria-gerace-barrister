Mediating and facilitating high conflict disputes

I have a keen interest in the facilitation and mediation of high conflict disputes.

Specialist skills are needed to assist people and organisations to navigate tricky or highly charged situations. Badly managed conflicts can have crippling financial, psychological and emotional consequences.

Litigation or conflicts arising from fraud, dishonest dealings, abuse, defective products, catastrophic personal injury or financial disaster are invariably highly charged.

Parties need legal strategies that recognise and are able to respond to the challenges of high conflict disputes.

Legal action to recover fraudulently appropriated funds

Victims of fraud seek to recovery stolen funds. Urgent legal action is often necessary to locate and 'freeze' funds before court action is taken to recover the misappropriated money. Delay in taking appropriate action may make recovery difficult as the proceeds of crime are frequently spent, moved overseas or 'disappear'. I am able to advise individual and corporate victims of fraud on legal action available to locate and recover stolen funds

Whistleblowers

Fraud, corruption, corporate wrongdoing, cover ups.

Corporate and social pressure make speaking up fraud, corruption, defective products and other wrongdoing, difficult.

Whistle blowers need early confidential advice and protection.

I have acted for a number of whistleblowers who have chosen to speak up about information they have about corporate and individual wrongdoing. I have represented and protected whistleblowers who have chosen to give evidence in various inquiries, special commissions and Royal Commissions. I have acted as Counsel Assisting to a Royal Commission that has taken evidence from whistleblowers.

If you have information about fraud, corruption or other corporate wrongdoing and need early confidential advice or representation, contact my chambers to arrange a conference to discuss.
